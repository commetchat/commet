<h3> Seamless multi-account support</h3>

<p> Commet was built from the ground up with multi-account support in mind, with a unique approach which doesn't require manually switching account.</p>

<p>All your accounts are logged in simultaneously, seamlessly blending together so you can focus on the conversation </p>

<h3>Permission management that makes sense</h3>

<p>Ever felt overwhelmed or confused by the permission system in Matrix? Commet makes it as simple as a drag and drop.</p>

<p>Matrix's permission system is heirarchical, so we display it that way. It's easy to understand at just a glance!</p>
