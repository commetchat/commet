    The goal is to build a secure, privacy respecting app without compromising on the features you have come to expect
    from a modern chat client.</p>

<h3>Your space to connect</h3>

<p>In addition to text and calls, Commet provides additional tools to help you hang out and connect with your friends.
    Easily plan events with a shared calendar, and upload all the photos to a shared album!</p>


<h3> Seamless multi-account support</h3>

<p> Commet was built from the ground up with multi-account support in mind, with a unique approach which doesn't require
    manually switching account.</p>

<p>All your accounts are logged in simultaneously, seamlessly blending together so you can focus on the conversation.
</p>
